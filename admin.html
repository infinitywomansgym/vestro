One-time setup needed

The admin portal saves your products online with Firebase (free). It takes about 10 minutes to connect.

How to connect

  1. Create a free Firebase project at console.firebase.google.com, enable Firestore and Email/Password login.
  2. Paste your Firebase config into firebase-config.js.
  3. Reload this page — the login box will appear.

Until then, your live site keeps showing the 4 starter sarees, so nothing is broken.
